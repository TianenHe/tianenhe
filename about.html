Tianen He

Illustrator BFA Student · MICA Baltimore, MD

Hello, I'm Tianen He, an illustration student at Maryland Institute College of Art. I prefer people to call me Amanda!

I enjoy creating my own original characters and building their stories. I also love following and collecting illustrators' work on social media, it makes me feel like I'm constantly learning and growing!

Anime, comics and games have always been a main motivation for me to keep drawing, and I often dream of one day creating my own comics or games. I'm meticulous and easy to work with!
